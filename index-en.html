About me

My Story: Bridging Technology and Business

My professional path connects two worlds: technology and business. It started with a fascination for sci-fi and building my own computers. It has always been key for me to understand systems in depth – not to become an expert, but to create a tool that serves me perfectly.

This passion paradoxically led me not to programming, but to business. With over ten years of experience leading complex B2B projects, I have personally experienced how the rules of the game have changed. Today, a great product is not enough; in an era when clients have unlimited access to information, the role of the salesperson has shifted from presenter to detective. My task is to listen, uncover hidden needs, and find solutions that truly work.

Bridge Between Business and Technology

I believe real value today is created at the intersection of different fields. That's why I actively seek insight into areas that traditionally don't belong in an Account Manager's portfolio:

  • I gain insight into AI and code. With tools like Cursor, I'm not just trying to peek under the hood of technology, but to understand the complex world of AI. The best way is to engage with AI and create – not with the goal of becoming a programmer, but as a businessperson to have deep technical knowledge that allows me to optimize solutions between development and the customer.
  • I have an overview of marketing and design. Thanks to several years in marketing companies and practical experience with tools like Premiere Pro, Photoshop, and Google, I have a comprehensive understanding of PPC, SEO, and other key factors. In my view, it's not enough to be just a salesperson with a phone, but also with a website.

My Vision: AI as an Amplifier of Human Potential

When artificial intelligence entered the scene, all the pieces of my puzzle fell perfectly into place. My passion for functional systems connected with a deep understanding of business. I don't see AI as a replacement for humans, but as their best assistant. It's a tool with the power to eliminate repetitive burden – data work, reporting, routine tasks – and free our hands for what we're irreplaceable in: creativity, strategic thinking, and building solid relationships with customers.

My goal is to lead projects that generate measurable profit for companies through smart and strategic integration of technology. I want to build systems that truly help people and allow them to focus on the core of their work.
